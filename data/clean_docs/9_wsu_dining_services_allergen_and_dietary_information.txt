What is NetNutrition®?
You can use NetNutrition® to:
View nutritional content and ingredients of your food choices
Filter out foods based on traits
Select preferences for specific types of diets like vegan, or Halal.
General navigation
How to Use NetNutrition® to Manage Your Food Allergy
Find food allergens/intolerances listed on the left side of the page.
Check the box next to the food allergens/intolerances you must avoid.
How to Use NetNutrition® in Meal Planning
Click on the box next to the food items you wish to eat.
Use the drop down box on the right to select the number of portions you wish to consume.
Once you have made your selections, select “Add Items”.
As you add items to your meal, they will appear in the upper left-hand shaded portion of the page under “My Meal”.
You may review nutritional information by the meal (select “Meal Nutrition”) or by individual item (select “Item Nutrition”).
To delete an item from your meal, select the red circle with a minus sign.
How to use NetNutrition® for Preferences
Select your desired preference.
How to use NetNutrition® on Your Mobile Device
Go to
NetNutrition
from your mobile device.
Select “Start”.
Select “Marketplace Dining”.
Select the desired date.
Select the funnel icon to filter out desired food allergens/intolerances.
Select the page icon to the right of the food to view nutritional information/ingredients.
