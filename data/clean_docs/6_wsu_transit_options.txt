Riding Pullman Transit can benefit you by avoiding the costs of operating and parking a vehicle, as well as driving during inclement weather. Public Transit also reduces congestion on campus roadways, and improves the quality of life by helping the university community support sustainability and green initiatives.
Park and Ride Options
Commuter travel can be accommodated with Park and Ride options.  Permits are not required in Park and Ride lots during the day.  The campus Park and Ride lot is located at the corner of Valley Road and Merman Drive, adjacent to the Valley Road Intramural Playfields. Follow this link to view the
WSU Pullman Campus Parking Map.
