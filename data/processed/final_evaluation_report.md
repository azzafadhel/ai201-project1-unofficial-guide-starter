## Question 2

**Question:** What should international students do for SEVIS check-in after arriving at WSU?

**Expected answer:** International students should complete the required SEVIS check-in process after arriving at WSU as part of their international student arrival requirements.

**System response:** International students are required to check in with SEVIS as a new student within their first week of being on campus. They can do this by logging in to "myPassport" using their WSU username and password and completing the new student check-in. Failure to do so will result in the termination of their SEVIS record and could have a negative impact on their immigration status.

**Accuracy judgment:** accurate

## Question 5

**Question:** How can students find vegan, vegetarian, halal, or allergen-friendly food at WSU Dining?

**Expected answer:** Students can use WSU Dining menu labels and NetNutrition to check ingredients, allergens, and dietary categories such as vegan, vegetarian, halal, gluten-friendly, and other food options.

**System response:** Students can use NetNutrition to find vegan, vegetarian, halal, or allergen-friendly food at WSU Dining. They can filter out foods based on traits, select preferences for specific types of diets, and view nutritional content and ingredients of their food choices. Registered dietitians are also on staff to assist with any concerns.
