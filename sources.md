# Sources for The Unofficial Guide

## Domain

WSU Campus Survival Guide for New and International Students

## Domain Summary

My domain is a WSU Campus Survival Guide for new and international students in Pullman. The guide focuses on practical student knowledge about arrival, transportation, dining, CougarCard use, banking, health resources, safety, winter preparation, and campus adjustment. This knowledge is hard to find in one place because official WSU pages are spread across many departments, while unofficial student advice is scattered across Reddit and student discussions.

## Overall Source Structure Notes

The source collection mixes official WSU pages with one unofficial student discussion. Most official sources are structured as guides, checklists, or informational webpages with clear headings and short paragraphs. The Reddit source is more informal and opinion-based, with shorter student comments and more varied viewpoints.

Because many documents are section-based, the later chunking strategy should preserve headings and group related paragraphs together. Long official pages should be split into medium chunks around topics such as transportation, dining, health, and safety, while Reddit comments can be kept as smaller chunks because each comment often expresses one student experience.
